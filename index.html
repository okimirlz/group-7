Introduction

We are Grade 10-2 students from APEC Schools Marikina Heights. The focus of our content lies within the common everyday topics that one may find interesting, such as what is currently happening, to more niche topics. Though, we first started the channel with the intention of researching and talking about Financial Instability. We have expanded to incorporate more of the topics around us. The reason for the incorporation of other topics stems from our name CHIKA

  1. Brainstorming the Topic

    August 29th, 2024

    We started thinking of a topic, that us as a group found interesting. We landed on a few things, but only a few stood out. The one that stood out was Poverty. Which we narrowed down to Poverty in Education.

  2. Creation of Name

    September 11th, 2024

    Due to another project we were working on, we had to create a name for it. The name Chika voted by the majority of our members, and has continued as is.

    The original plan for CHIKA was to get people that experience Financial Instability, to "CHIKA" or share their experiences with us. This helps their voices to be heard by the audience, and it helps us to better understand the issue from different perspectives of those who experience it. But, we decided to expand the scope of our topics, as to commit to the name CHIKA.

  3. Creation of Chika's Logo

    September 16th, 2024

    We brainstormed about the colors that would be present in the logo and other things. Once we were done, one of the members created the logo. Although it isn't the one present today.

  4. Audience Analysis

    September 17th, 2024

    We started to create our google forms and to have others answer it, in order to gather data.

  5. Analysis of Data from the Survey Form

    October 6th, 2024

    We started to analyze the data we have received from the form we sent out. We have found a multitude of things that have allowed us to better understand the topic, and how it affects our fellow students.

  6. Finalization of Topic

    October 14th, 2024

    Based on the feedback of our advisor, she has suggested for us to become more specific with our topic as poverty is still a wide topic on its own. We decided to become more specific and agreed for the topic to be about Financial Instability. As for the audience analysis, the previous responds already answered the topic of Financial Instability.

  7. Planning of Stream Design, Color Scheme, and Fonts

    November 20th, 2024

    As per the lesson of the week, we started to create the design of our stream overlay using the fonts we have chosen. The fonts we chose were the ones that we thought were the best and fitted our content the most. As for the color scheme, we simply chose to use the color scheme of our Logo.

  8. Planning of Website Layout

    November 27th, 2024

    We brainstormed and started to plan out the design and layout of the website, that we will follow for the coding. The overview of the website was designed with animations to create a lively and engaging experience while maintaining a clean layout. It includes features like smooth transitions and well-structured content that balances functionality with visual appeal.

  9. Creation of Promotional Plan & Schedule Outline

    December 4th, 2024

    We started to create the outline of the schedule of our stream along with our promotional plan. The outline of the schedule consists of the details that we would be helpful to us in our stream. And our promotional plan contains the details of how we will promote our channel along with the drafts and final product of our poster.

  10. Revision & Finalization of the Logo

    December 7th, 2024

    We started to revise our Logo due to it containing redundant text and designs that we put before in Quarter 1. We removed this redundancy and continued on to finalizing it. This is the current Logo present in our platforms and website.

  11. Creation of Streaming Platform Accounts

    December 9th, 2024

    Facebook was our choice of streaming platform, and we created our first account and page on it. We are still currently fixing things within it and plan to post our poster by December 12.
